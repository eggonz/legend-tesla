v0.0 - First Version

For the moment, this is the first and only version of the game. This version shows game basics and main overworld mechanics, although battle mechanichs have not been implemented. Yet, it may be considered as a first approach to future more complete versions. Its development started on November 2019, and was finally released on February 2020.
